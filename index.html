  • Michigan Milk Producers Association

    Cloverdale Farms is a Proud MMPA Member

    The Michigan Milk Producers Association is a dairy farmer owned cooperative and dairy processor. Founded in 1916, the MMPA serves dairy farmers in Michigan, Indiana, Ohio and Wisconsin.

  • Michigan Farm Bureau Awards

    Farm Bureau Achievement in Agriculture Finalist

    Owner-Operator Garrett Bartholomew was selected as a finalist out of many applicants for Michigan Farm Bureau's Achievement in Agricultu award. This award recognizes a succesful young agriculturalist or couple in the Young Farmer & Rancher program.

  • Some History

    Humans have been drinking milk from cows for thousands of years. Modern dairy farming began in the early 1900’s after pasteurization was developed and utilized widely. Pasteurization allows for a safer product and extends milk’s shelf life by eradicating spoilage-causing bacteria through the application of heat. This process allows milk to last longer and be shipped further.
